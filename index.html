About me

I'm Christian de Guzman, a NASM certified trainer specializing in strength training and powerlifting. My own transformation story is one of incredible perseverance and dedication. Starting at 90 kilograms, I embarked on a weight loss journey while simultaneously getting stronger, ultimately reaching a lean and powerful 67.5 kilograms. Along the way, I discovered my passion for powerlifting and competed in several exhilarating competitions.

Through my personal experiences, I've gained a deep understanding of the challenges and triumphs that come with achieving fitness goals. I take joy in seeing myself break my own personal records at several powerlifting meets. As a coach, I bring this firsthand knowledge to my clients, both male and female, who have achieved remarkable progress under my guidance. I've witnessed athletes break their own records and make incredible strides towards their fitness aspirations.

My coaching approach is centered around providing personalized training programs, tailored nutrition advice, and unwavering support. I believe in fostering a positive and motivating environment where my clients feel empowered to push their limits and redefine what they thought was possible. Whether you're a beginner on a quest to improve your strength or an experienced powerlifter aiming for new heights, I am here to help you unlock your full potential. Join me on this journey, and together, we'll exceed your expectations in the world of strength training and powerlifting.
